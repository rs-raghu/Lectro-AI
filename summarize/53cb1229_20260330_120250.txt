### What is Machine Learning?
*   A branch of Artificial Intelligence (AI).
*   Enables systems to learn from data and improve performance over time.
*   Distinguished by not requiring explicit programming.

### How Machine Learning Works
*   Utilizes algorithms to identify patterns within data.

### Key Applications & Advantages
*   Enables predictions and automation of complex tasks.
*   **Examples:** Fraud detection, recommendation systems.
*   Excels where traditional rule-based programming is inefficient.
