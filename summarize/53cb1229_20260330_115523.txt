### Embedded Systems: Core Definition

*   **Specialized Computing Device:** An embedded system is a computing device built for a very specific purpose.
*   **Hardware-Software Integration:** It combines both hardware and software components.
*   **Dedicated Function:** Designed to perform one particular function or set of functions.

### Key Characteristics & Distinctions

*   **Integrated Role:** Unlike general-purpose computers (like PCs), embedded systems are integrated *within* a larger mechanical or electronic system.
*   **Specific Task Control:** Its primary role is to control specific, dedicated tasks within that larger system.
