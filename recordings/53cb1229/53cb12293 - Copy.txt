Ok, let's start with chapter number 4. The Sky Beyond the Universe If even the higher planets in this universe are subject to birth and death, why do greats, yogis, strive for elevation to them? Although they may have many mystic powers, these yogis still have the tendency to want to enjoy the facilities of material life. On the higher planets, it is possible to live for incredibly long lifetimes. The time calculation on these planets is indicated by Sri Krishna.
